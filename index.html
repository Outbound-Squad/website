Infrastructure

Optimize the tech stack and make execution simple.

  • Audit your tools. Most teams have 8+ tools that don't talk to each other.
  • Reduce friction. If it takes a rep 15 clicks to make a call, they won't call.
  • Align your tech to your methodology, not the other way around.
